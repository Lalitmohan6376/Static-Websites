🥗 Healthy Nutrition Guide

Proper nutrition is the foundation of a healthy body and fitness journey. What you eat directly affects your energy, muscle growth, and overall health.

💪 Protein

Protein helps in muscle building and recovery. Include eggs, chicken, paneer, dal, and soybeans in your diet.

🍚 Carbohydrates

Carbs provide energy for workouts and daily activities. Eat rice, oats, whole wheat, bananas, and potatoes.

🥑 Healthy Fats

Healthy fats support brain function and hormones. Use nuts, seeds, olive oil, and avocado.

💧 Hydration

Drink at least 2–3 liters of water daily to stay hydrated and active.

🔥 Daily Nutrition Tips

  • Eat balanced meals (Protein + Carbs + Fats)
  • Avoid junk food and sugary drinks
  • Eat small meals every 3–4 hours
  • Include fruits and vegetables daily
  • Sleep properly for better digestion and recovery
